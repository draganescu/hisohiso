Hisohiso is private chat stripped back to first principles: no accounts, no profiles, no phone-number moat, no cloud archive pretending to be convenience. You open a room, share a link, people knock, someone inside approves, and the conversation happens in a space that exists only for the people already in it. Messages are encrypted in the browser, history stays on each device, and any participant can kill the room entirely. It is not a social network, not a collaboration suite, and not another identity game disguised as messaging. It is just a temporary, self-hostable room for people who already know why they need to talk.
